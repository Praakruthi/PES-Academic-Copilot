# PES Academic Copilot

## Vision

PES Academic Copilot is a browser extension that helps PES students find, understand, and study course materials efficiently using AI.

The goal is to reduce the time students spend searching through notes, slides, assignments, and question banks while improving exam preparation and concept understanding.

---

## Problem Statement

PES students have access to a large number of academic resources through PES Academy, including notes, slides, assignments, and question banks.

However, finding relevant resources for a specific topic is often time-consuming. Students must manually open multiple files, search through documents, and organize study materials.

There is currently no intelligent system that allows students to search across all academic resources using natural language.

---

## Target Users

* PES students
* Students preparing for ISA and ESA examinations
* Students who want quick access to study materials
* Students who maintain their own collection of notes and previous-year question papers

---

## Core Features (MVP)

### Resource Discovery

Students can search for study materials related to a topic.

Example:
"Show all resources related to Deadlocks."

### AI Question Answering

Students can ask questions directly from course materials.

Example:
"Explain Round Robin Scheduling."

### Local File Uploads

Students can upload PDFs, PPTX files, and DOCX files.

Example:
Previous-year question papers, senior notes, handwritten notes converted to PDF.

### Summarization

Students can generate concise summaries of topics and documents.

### MCQ Generation

Students can generate practice questions from study materials.

### Quiz Mode

Students can test themselves through an interactive question-and-answer session.

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if a PES student can:

1. Install the extension.
2. Index their course materials.
3. Search for resources using natural language.
4. Ask questions from study materials.
5. Upload personal study materials.
6. Generate summaries and MCQs.
7. Use the extension as a study companion during exam preparation.
